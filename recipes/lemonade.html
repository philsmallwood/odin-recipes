Alec's Lemonade

Description

This is recipe that my son, Alec, developed for his favorite lemonade

It's super simple, and has lots of flavor

Ingredients

  • 5-8 Lemons
  • 1 Lime
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 5 Cups Water

Steps

  1. Start by squeezing all of the juice of the lemons and lime into a carafe or pitcher.
  2. Make a simple syrup by boiling 1 cup of the water and the cup of sugar
  3. After the simple syrup is clear, grate the zest of the lime and 1 lemon into the syrup
  4. After the simple syrup cools, pour 3/4 of a cup into the carafe/pitcher
  5. Add 3 cups of water to the pitcher/carafe
  6. Add a little more water or simple syrup to taste
  7. Cool in the refridgerator for an hour before enjoying
