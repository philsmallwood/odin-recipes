Phil's Favorite PB&J

Ingredients

  • Crunchy Peanut Butter
  • Strawberry Jelly
  • Homestyle White Bread

Steps

  1. Take two pieces of hearty white bread.
  2. Spread a generous amount of Crunch PeanutButter on one slice.
  3. Spread Strawberry Jelly on the other slice. Use just enough to barely cover the bread.
  4. Put the slices together. Cut on the diagonal.
