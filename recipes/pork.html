Aiden's Pork 'Stew'

Ingredients

  • Two (2) Pork Tenderloins
  • One (1) Bag Baby Carrots
  • Five (5) Medium Red Potatoes
  • One (1) Yellow Onion
  • Splash of Red Wine
  • Spices (salt, pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, rosemary)

Steps

  1. Chop onion and potatoes into smallish pieces
  2. Put onions, potatoes, and baby carrots into a crock pot
  3. Brown the tenderloins in a pan with a little oil
  4. Place tenderloins over the potato, carrot, onion mixture
  5. Splash with red wine
  6. Season liberally with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and rosemary
  7. Cook on low for 5.5 hours
